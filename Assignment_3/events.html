Competitions & Events

Below is a list of the main events. Use the registration page to sign up.

  1. Hackathon — 24-hour team hackathon (max 4 per team). Prizes for top 3.
  2. Coding Contest — Individual competitive programming (3 rounds).
  3. Robotics Challenge — Line follower / obstacle course (teams of 3).
  4. Quiz Bee — General knowledge & technical rounds (teams of 4).
  5. Design & Poster — Individual digital poster submission.
  6. Music & Dance — Cultural performances (open auditions).
  7. Sports Meet — Football, Badminton & Table Tennis events.

For rules, prizes and eligibility see our FAQs on the Contact page.
